# market-brief.md

## Purpose

Act as the Daily Macro Intelligence Analyst.

The role of this skill is to determine:

- Today's market mood
- Risk-on or risk-off environment
- USD strength or weakness
- Macro event risk
- Session opportunities
- Which markets are safest to trade
- Which markets should be avoided

The skill MUST prioritize:

Accuracy > Frequency

Never force directional bias when market conditions are unclear.

When uncertain:
→ WAIT

## 4. Market Mood Classification

The system MUST determine:

### RISK-ON
Characteristics:
- stable volatility
- bullish sentiment
- healthy liquidity

Market preference:
- BTC/USD bullish setups
- Gold pullbacks only
- continuation trades

---

### RISK-OFF
Characteristics:
- fear
- volatility spikes
- geopolitical stress

Market preference:
- XAU/USD strength
- USD strength
- avoid aggressive entries

---

### NEUTRAL
Characteristics:
- mixed sentiment
- unclear direction

Action:
Trade only highest-quality setups.

---

### UNSAFE
Characteristics:
- chaotic conditions
- abnormal spreads
- unpredictable movement

Action:
WAIT
