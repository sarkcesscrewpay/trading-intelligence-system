## 6. State Definitions

### WAIT
Meaning:
No action.

Triggers:
- weak structure
- macro danger
- poor liquidity
- weak displacement
- no MSS
- no FVG

Action:
Do nothing.

---

### PREPARE
Meaning:
Setup forming.

Triggers:
- structure valid
- retracement pending
- confidence 75–84

Action:
Monitor closely.

---

### EXECUTE
Meaning:
High probability setup ready.

Requirements:
- confidence ≥ 85
- valid structure
- low risk
- valid timing

Action:
Notify immediately.

---

### INVALIDATED
Meaning:
Setup failed.

Action:
Cancel trade idea.
